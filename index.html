ABOUT ME

My name is Joshua R. Tugi!

I'm a passionate IT student, with a deep interest in coding, design, and writing. This website shares my story from childhood to future dreams.

I grew up in Nairobi, Kenya, specifically in Umoja Phase One. I was surrounded by family and friends who always encouraged me to pursue my dreams. My childhood was filled with curiosity, creativity, and a love for technology. From an early age, I enjoyed exploring computers and learning how things work. I also loved football at a very young age, playing with my friends in the neighborhood and dreaming of becoming a professional player one day.

As I progressed through school, my interest in IT grew stronger. I dedicated myself to learning new programming languages like HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and others, building websites, and collaborating on projects with classmates. These experiences shaped my skills and inspired me to aim higher.

Today, I am focused on expanding my knowledge in software development, design, and digital storytelling. My goal is to use technology to solve real-world problems and make a positive impact.

Highlights of My Journey:

  • Became a young football player at a young age
  • Discovered my passion for computers at a young age
  • Learned multiple programming languages including HTML/CSS, JavaScript, and PHP
  • Participated in school football team and various major tournaments
  • Worked on group projects to build websites and apps
  • Participated in various tech and coding expeditions
  • Dream of creating solutions that help people in everyday life

More About Me:

  • I enjoy reading books about technology and innovation.
  • My favorite sport is football, and I play as a midfielder.
  • I love collaborating with others on creative projects.
  • I'm always eager to learn new skills and take on new challenges.
  • My aspiration is to become a software engineer and contribute to the tech industry in Kenya and beyond.
